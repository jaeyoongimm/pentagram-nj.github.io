About the Project

Premised as a literal inversion of the classic white box gallery into an independent, freestanding, small-sized architectural object, Gallery Attachment questions perceived notions of exhibition and public space, creating an opportunity for a new conduit of architectural thinking for architectural allies and strangers alike. Gallery Attachment aims to be both container and content; exhibition space and object; art gallery and public space. Gallery Attachment is a small museum constructed out of the debris and discarded elements of the contemporary, physical and cultural environment.
